# Materials Used

- Two sterilized containers (one copper, one plastic/glass)
- Tap water or any common water source
- Sterile paper discs or small paper cut-outs
- Agar plates (Nutrient Agar)
- Sterile cotton swabs
- Incubator or warm dark space
- Sterile dropper or pipette
- Gloves and mask (for hygiene)
- Marker, ruler, notebook
